# Collapse Cluster

## Definition

The **Collapse Cluster** characterizes rapid systemic breakdown where complex societies lose institutional capacity and revert to simpler organizational forms.

---

```
IF (V-01 AND V-02) AND (C-01 OR C-03) AND (MP-02)
```
